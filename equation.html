Balancing Equations Game

How to Play
Drag the correct chemical formulas from the coloured cards into the blank boxes to complete the chemical reaction. Each equation must appear in one line as: Reactant + Reactant → Product + Product. Click Check Answer to see if your placements are correct. Correct answers turn green. Incorrect answers turn red. You may drag another card to replace an incorrect answer. Click Skip Question to move to the next reaction. Click Show Answer to reveal the correct formulas.
